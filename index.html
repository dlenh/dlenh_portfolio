Hello! My name is David Lenh.

I am a San Francisco based Full-Stack Software Engineer with a background in scientific research and education. While attending the University of California, San Diego for a Masters in Biology, I was a teaching assistant (TA). After graduation, I continued the education track and became a tutor. Throughout my years as a TA and tutor, I have used software that often malfunctioned and was simply not up to par. It flustered me, my coworkers, and even my students. I felt like that was something I can improve on, and so I picked up learning code in my free time.

What I Do

Here to provide you with

web presence.

Consultation

It starts with a conversation going over your initial vision for the web presence you want to have. It can be building a website from the ground up, or updating an outdated presence for the mobile era.

Proposal

Based on our consultation, I will draft a detailed project proposal that includes a visual preview, estimated costs, and a projected timeline outlining the plan of action.

Development

Clients will receive regularly scheduled updates regarding their project status while I create and test it. Your feedback will be taken into consideration to bring your site to life.

Launch & Support

Once the site is finished and you are completely satisfied, we will launch your web presence. Maintenance fees may apply post-production to ensure smooth operation of your site.
